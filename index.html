FAQ

Questions Answered

Is CapCut professional enough for serious content? +
Absolutely. Top creators worldwide — including MrBeast's team and major Indian Reels creators — use CapCut. The tool doesn't define the quality; the editor does. I focus on results. I'm also actively learning DaVinci Resolve and After Effects for clients who want even more advanced work.
When do I pay? +
50% advance to start the project, and 50% when I deliver the final video. Payment via UPI — instant and hassle-free. No international payment complications.
What if I don't like the edit? +
Every package includes free revisions. Share your feedback and I'll make the changes — no drama, no extra charges. My goal is for you to love the final result.
Can we work together long-term? +
Yes, and I'd love that. Monthly bulk packages are available at a better per-video rate than single bookings. Long-term clients also get priority slots and faster turnaround.
Which platforms do you edit for? +
Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, WhatsApp Status, and general promos or brand videos. I export in the correct aspect ratio and format for each platform automatically.
How do I send you my footage? +
Google Drive or WhatsApp — whichever is easier for you. If your files are large, Drive is usually faster. I'll guide you through the process after we confirm the project.
